Miotais Wilberry

Can you uncover the myths of the town?
Could it be the Myths are a lie or could they be true?

Miotias Wilberry is a horror Puzzle Game, that the player must find a way out of the woods by finding keys to open doors,notes to help them. The player needs to try and stay safe and manage to escape the woods and tell the tale of the woods surronding Wilberry. Are the Myths true?

The player will find out about the myths of the town that they live in and they will hopefully be able to work out what happened to all the people of the town who entered the woods but never came out. If they do escape they have to find away to the whole town and make them belive them as the myths have just been a childrens tale for decades now
